🗓 March 28, 2026

I Finally Understand CSS Grid!!

Okay so CSS Grid used to scare me SO much but today it finally clicked. The trick was thinking of it like a garden plot — you lay out rows and columns like planting beds and then put things in them. I even made a little grid visualizer to help me learn. Maybe I'll share it as a project soon!

mood: 🌟 excited & proud
🗓 March 20, 2026

My Calculator Project Is Done!

After two weeks of work, my calculator app is finally finished! It was harder than I thought — especially handling the order of operations and making the buttons feel clicky and satisfying. I learned a lot about event listeners and how to organize my code better.

mood: 🎉 accomplished
🗓 March 12, 2026

Why I Love Coding

Sometimes people ask me why I like coding so much and honestly? It's like building with invisible LEGOs. You start with nothing and then suddenly there's this thing on the screen that YOU made and it WORKS and it's the best feeling ever. Also the debugging is like solving puzzles which is fun (most of the time lol).
